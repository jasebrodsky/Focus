Why Focus?

Dating in today’s digital world has become more about matching with others than about connecting with others. We designed an expirence where focusing on connection is more important. People who you match with will be revealed again as messages are exchanged and connections are made. We believe that focusing on less creates better connections.

How Focus works.

Swipe on who you like.

If they like you back you'll be able to message each other, but their photos will not be focused.

Focus on each other.

With every message exchanged, photos will focus, revealing again how you both look.

Create real Connections.

This creates deeper meaningful connections based on who you are instead of what you look like.
